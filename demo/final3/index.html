Alone, Shared, or Other?

How daily time divides between solitary, social, and other contexts across survey years. Each square represents one percentage point.

Alone activities include computing, cleaning, and commuting. Shared activities include socialising, entertainment, childcare, walking, and employment. Classification derived from the modal social context of each activity across all survey waves, ignoring unclassified entries.

Data: Eurostat HETUS (Harmonised European Time Use Surveys) · Covers participating European countries across three survey waves (2000, 2010, 2020)
